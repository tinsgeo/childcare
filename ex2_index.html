Our Age-Tailored Programs

Infant Program

6 weeks – 18 months
  • Individualized schedules matching home routine
  • Sensory play, sign language, music & movement
  • Focus on attachment and brain development
  • Daily infant massage and gentle yoga
  • Breast milk & formula support

Toddler Program

18 months – 3 years
  • Positive potty training support
  • Hands-on art, blocks, and dramatic play
  • Introduction to letters, numbers, shapes
  • Daily outdoor play and gross motor fun
  • Focus on independence & emotional growth

Preschool & Pre-K

3 – 5 years
  • Play-based curriculum with state standards
  • Phonics, storytelling, and writing centers
  • STEM experiments and beginner coding
  • Spanish, music, yoga, and mindfulness
  • Kindergarten readiness portfolio
